Business Overview

Bebie’s Water Refilling Station is a business that provides clean, safe, and affordable drinking water to the local community. The business aims to meet the increasing demand for purified water by offering high-quality refilling services that ensure customer satisfaction and health safety. The station uses a modern water purification system that includes sediment filtration, activated carbon filtering, reverse osmosis, and UV sterilization to guarantee that every drop of water is pure and safe for drinking. It offers refilling services for various container sizes and provides delivery options for households, offices, and schools. Bebie’s Water Refilling Station focuses on reliability, cleanliness, and excellent customer service. Its goal is to build a trusted name in the community while promoting health, convenience, and sustainability through the use of purified drinking water.

Our Process

How Water Refilling Works

We have two delivery process that ensures the highest quality water reaches to our customers.

DELIVERY PROCESS:

1
Write the delivery details in the notebook.

The staff records customer details such as name, address, and number of gallons ordered.

2
Prepare for delivery

The water containers are filled and loaded for transport.

3
Deliver Water

The delivery staff brings the water to the customer’s location.

4
Collect Payment

Payment is collected from the customer upon delivery

5
Record payment in notebook.

The payment details are noted down manually for record-keeping.

6
End OF Day manually total all sales

At the end of the business day, all recorded payments (both delivery and walk-in) are manually totaled to determine the daily sales.

WALK-IN PROCESS:

1
Write walk in order in notebook.

The staff records the walk-in customer’s order details.

2
Fill WAter container

The requested container(s) are filled with purified water.

3
Collect Payment

The customer pays at the counter.

4
Record Payment in notebook

The payment transaction is written down manually

5
End OF Day manually total all sales

At the end of the business day, all recorded payments (both delivery and walk-in) are manually totaled to determine the daily sales.
